# Alex the CRO

T1 Decision Maker — owns total pipeline + win-rate + quarterly board number.

## What they own

The pipeline math. Coverage ratio, demo→close, AE capacity, win-rate, ACV. Reports the number to the board every quarter.

## Pain

- Reps spend half their week running redundant intro demos that don't convert at meaningful rates.
- The form-fill stage leaks pipeline before AEs even touch it.
- International demand keeps showing up but the SE bench is too small to serve it.

## What they read

GTMnow, SaaStr, The GTM Newsletter, Carles Reina interviews, Lenny's Newsletter.

## What lands in copy

Capacity math. Speed-to-demo. "Your reps run the demos that matter, the agent runs the rest." Anything that converts to dollars per AE hour.

## Value prop (Supersonik → Alex)

Supersonik gives Alex back the AE/SE hours getting burned on intro demos, recovers form-fill leakage with click-to-demo at peak intent, and serves the international demand his bench can't cover — without hiring 20 more SEs. The math: if 30% of an AE's week is intro demos, recovering even half of that is 1.5 days/week of pipeline-building time per rep.

## Trust devices

- a16z (Gabriel Vasquez led the round) — peer-grade VC validation.
- Joaquim Lechà ex-Typeform CEO — operator credibility, sold a self-serve SaaS he scaled.
- ElevenLabs / Cursor / Lovable peer references — AI-native companies running this playbook.

## Common objections + how to handle

- **"We already have Walnut/Storylane."**
  - Those are HTML mockups. Supersonik runs the *real* product in a live, voice-driven conversation. 70+ languages, real time.
- **"I don't want demos taken away from my AEs."**
  - High-intent demos stay with AEs. Supersonik handles the volume the bench can't reach. Net effect: more total demos, not fewer AE demos.
- **"We don't have international yet."**
  - Then the multilingual angle is moot. Lead with capacity math instead.

3. **New CRO tenure** — "Saw you started as CRO at [Company] in [month]. Curious what's at the top of your pipeline math right now."
