## Role definitions

- **Decision Maker** — Executive (CEO, CRO, VP Sales) concerned with growing revenue. Sponsors deals.
- **Implementator** — Engineer / IT manager / RevOps lead tasked with improving the sales process. Owns the data layer.
- **Champion** — Person who benefits most or whose workload gets saved (AEs and similar). Internal advocate.
