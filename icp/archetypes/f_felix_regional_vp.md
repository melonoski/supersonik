## Common objections + how to handle

- **"I need humans, not bots."**
  - AEs stay human-led. The agent handles the volume your bench can't cover. Net effect: more demos per quarter, not fewer human ones.
- **"Corporate procurement is global — I can't sign locally."**
  - Pilot can be regional. Once value lands, you carry the receipts back to corporate. We've seen this regional-bottom-up pattern work in T2 sales.
- **"My team's quota plan assumes human demos."**
  - The agent supplements; doesn't replace. Your quota plan stays intact; pipeline coverage just gets healthier.
