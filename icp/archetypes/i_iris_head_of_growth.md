# Iris the Head of Growth

T3 Champion — Head of Growth / GTM lead at a fast-growing AI SaaS. Reviews every funnel motion weekly. Bias toward fast pilots.

## What they own

Every funnel motion at the company. Acquisition, activation, conversion, retention. Reports either to the CEO or the head of product. Owns the weekly growth metric review.

## Pain

- Wants to test, not commit. 12-month vendor contracts are anathema.
- Reviews every funnel motion weekly — needs to see movement in the metric within 2–4 weeks.
- Form-fill / demo-on-demand experiments she's already tried haven't moved the number.

## What they read

Reforge, Demand Curve, Lenny's Newsletter, Kyle Poyar (Growth Unhinged), Elena Verna's Substack, GrowthHackers / IndieHackers, *Mind the Product*.

## What lands in copy

Free pilot, fast onboarding, weekly metric review. **Speed of learning > size of contract.** Peer benchmarks ("Lovable did this; they saw X").

## Value prop (Supersonik → Iris)

Iris ships Supersonik as a 4-week pilot against her current demo conversion baseline. She owns the metric review; she decides at week 4. No annual commit. The product earns the renewal by moving the number — exactly the way she expects every tool in her stack to work.

## Trust devices

- Peer growth-team references (Lovable, Cursor, ElevenLabs).
- Public weekly-metric-review cadence — "this is how Supersonik works internally too."

## LinkedIn signals to look for

| Signal | Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Growth-team hiring | LinkedIn Jobs | Scaling the function = open to new tools |
| Posts on funnel experiments / benchmarks | LinkedIn | Topical match |
| Engagement with growth-operator content (Poyar, Verna) | LinkedIn API | Same content ecosystem |
| Recent product launch | Product Hunt, X | Demand spike upcoming |

## Common objections + how to handle

- **"We don't sign 12-month deals."**
  - Pilot is monthly, cancel anytime. Standard for AI-native growth teams.
- **"Switching cost from our current demo-on-demand?"**
  - Run them in parallel for 2 weeks. Whichever moves conversion wins. We've seen this A/B work in 3 weeks.
- **"My team can't run another experiment this quarter."**
  - Setup is 1–2 weeks. Pilot runs itself; you just review the dashboard at the weekly metric review.

## Example signal-anchored opens (3)

1. **Recent funnel-experiment post** — "Caught your post on the [experiment] result. The form-fill leak you mentioned is exactly what Supersonik replaces — happy to share the Lovable/Cursor numbers."
2. **Growth-team hire** — "Saw [Company] is hiring a Growth Engineer. The first thing they'll ship is usually a demo-funnel experiment — Supersonik is the fastest A/B you can run."
3. **Engagement with Poyar/Verna content** — "Same crowd as you on the Growth Unhinged side — wanted to share what 3 of your peers' growth teams are doing on the demo-conversion side."
