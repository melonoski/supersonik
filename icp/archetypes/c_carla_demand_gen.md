# Carla the Demand Gen Lead

T1 Champion — owns MQL → SQL conversion and cost-per-pipeline. Her best traffic dies at the form-fill stage. Supersonik directly recovers her funnel leakage — she's the internal advocate.

## What they own

The top of the funnel through the handoff to sales. Channel mix, ad spend efficiency, landing-page conversion, MQL volume, MQL→SQL rate, cost-per-pipeline.

## Pain

- The form-fill is where her best traffic dies. People who clicked "Request a demo" disappear into the abyss.
- She's tested live chat (Drift/Intercom) and demo-on-demand (Consensus, Walnut). Neither is a *real* demo at peak intent.
- Cost-per-pipeline keeps drifting up. Every quarter the same conversion delta gets harder to find.

## What they read

Demand Curve, Growth Memo (Elena Verna), Kyle Poyar's *Growth Unhinged*, the HubSpot blog, MarketingProfs.

## What lands in copy

Conversion math. Specifically: peak-intent capture. The 32% interactive-demo conversion-lift category benchmark from `../../../04_industry.md`. The 75% rep-free preference. "Click-to-demo replaces form-fill."

## Value prop (Supersonik → Carla)

Carla replaces the form-fill leak with a click-to-demo that captures peak intent. Her best traffic stops dying at the bottom of the funnel — it converts into a live conversation with the real product, instantly, in the visitor's language. The math: a 3-percentage-point lift on form-to-meeting conversion at her traffic volume is hundreds of pipeline meetings per quarter.

## Trust devices

- Category benchmarks (RevenueHero, Vanta, M Accelerator) — cite them, don't invent Supersonik-specific lifts.
- Peer companies running this playbook (ElevenLabs, Lovable).

## LinkedIn signals to look for

| Signal | Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Multilingual landing pages (≥3 langs) | HTTP scrape | International funnel = multilingual demo win |
| "Request a demo" form on homepage | HTTP scrape | Form-fill is the current leak |
| Recent posts on form-fill / conversion optimization | LinkedIn | Topical interest |
| Heavy LinkedIn/Meta ad spend | LinkedIn Ad Library | Paying premium to drive form-fills that die |

## Common objections + how to handle

- **"We're already running Drift / Intercom for live chat."**
  - Live chat answers questions. Supersonik runs the actual demo — at the moment of peak intent.
- **"Our visitors prefer self-serve."**
  - 75% of B2B buyers prefer rep-free buying — until they hit a question they need answered. That's where the agent catches them.
- **"We don't have engineering capacity to integrate."**
  - It's a script tag plus a CRM mapping. Standard 1–2 week setup.

## Example signal-anchored opens (3)

1. **Demo form on homepage** — "Your homepage demo button still routes through a 4-step form. Click-to-demo lifts form-to-meeting by ~3 percentage points (RevenueHero, 2025)."
2. **Multilingual landing pages** — "You're running landing pages in 5 languages. The demo behind those pages is still English-only — closing that gap is the highest-ROI move in your funnel."
3. **LinkedIn ad-spend signal** — "Saw you're scaling LinkedIn ads to [persona]. Curious what your form-to-meeting rate is — that's almost always where ad-driven pipeline disappears."
