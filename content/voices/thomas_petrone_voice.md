- **Parallel-structure triplet** as a rhythmic peak ("Five minutes. / Hundreds of MBA students. / One mission: ...").

- **Growth metric anchor** ("we plan to 4x our headcount this year") — signals momentum.
- **Two-step CTA** (apply via LinkedIn OR email Dale OR DM me) — multiple paths, all clear.
- **Brand-orange in-joke emoji** (🟧) for insider moments.

## Anti-patterns / What Not to Write

- Posts without an action CTA — every Thomas post should pull someone toward a job, market, or conversation.
- Reflective or sentimental closes — not his voice. "Let's speak" / "Let's keep building" is the close.
- Statesman gracious-thanks framing — Joaquim's lane.
- Technical-mechanism observations — Pol's lane.
- Generic "I'm thrilled to be at [Event]" without a market insight + recruiting ask.
- Heavy emoji clusters (more than 3 in a sequence except flags).
- Long-form first-person reflection — keeps it crisp and action-oriented.
